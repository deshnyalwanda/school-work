About Us

We are a small group of students based in Nairobi, Kenya, passionate about making the use of AI as seamless as possible. We identified a common challenge: converting AI-generated mathematical text, often in LaTeX or similar formats, into easily editable Word documents. This is our first application, and we hope to continue building tools that simplify complex tasks for students and professionals alike. We are committed to providing a reliable and user-friendly service.
